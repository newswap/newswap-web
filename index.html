Economic Model

  • The token price in NewSwap is set by system and a transaction fee of 0.3% will be charged for all trades occurring in NewSwap. All fees are provided to liquidity providers of the pool.
  • When liquidity mining is launched, the transaction fee of 0.25% will be rewarded directly to liquidity providers. The transaction fee of 0.05% will go into Liquidity Reward Pool to support the value of NewSwap Token (NST).
  • Adding liquidity on specified pools to mine NST by staking LP tokens and NewSwap Power(NSP). Also, staking NSP into governance contracts could mine NST.
